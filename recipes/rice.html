RICE RECIPE

DESCRIPTION

Rice, (Oryza sativa), edible starchy cereal grain and the grass plant (family Poaceae) by which it is produced. Roughly one-half of the world population, including virtually all of East and Southeast Asia, is wholly dependent upon rice as a staple food; 95 percent of the world's rice crop is eaten by humans.

INGREDIENT

  • 1 1/4 cups Kellogg's Rice KrispiesÂ® cereal
  • 2 tablespoons sliced almonds
  • 2 tablespoons semi-sweet chocolate morsels
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup fat-free milk or chocolate milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla

STEPS

  1. Place KELLOGG'S RICE KRISPIES cereal in serving bowl.
  2. Arrange almond slices and chocolate morsels in concentric circles on cereal. Sprinkle with cinnamon
  3. Stir together milk and vanilla. Serve milk mixture with cereal.
